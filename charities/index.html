Charities/Causes

“Happiness doesn’t result from what we get, but from what we give.”

-Ben Carson

We here at CloudHack Technologies Believe that what you give is what you get.

CloudHack Technologies is a proud supporter of:

THE BILL AND MELINDA GATES FOUNDATION

The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation works to help all people lead healthy, productive lives. In developing countries, it focuses on improving people’s health and giving them the chance to lift themselves out of hunger and extreme poverty. In the United States, it seeks to ensure that all people—especially those with the fewest resources—have access to the opportunities they need to succeed in school and life.

HOW WE HELP:

We support the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation by donating directly through the Gates Philanthropy Partners each month. Then we donate to the Bill and Melinda Gate grantees that we believe will make the most positive difference in our world such as the United States Fund for UNICEF. We also donate our used technology to the Bill and Melinda Gates grantees.
