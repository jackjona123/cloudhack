Our Story

Our founder, Jack Jona, back in 2013, was working on creating a storage server that was accessible over the network for his house. When he finished deploying the server he realized how easy and simple it was to have accessible data over the internet. That is when he had the idea of creating a cloud computing company that could offer cloud service from his home to his customers. At first, he created software that would securely and efficiently manage his cloud storage service, which was the only service that he offered at the time. He named his company CloudHack Technologies. His first customers were his family members and his relatives who wanted their data to be stored securely in the “cloud,” then they recommended to their friends, and his customers began to grow. Eventually, he had enough customers to the point where he could not manage his service alone, and he hired two people at first. He expanded his team to four members and relocated where Jack and his team set out to expand their users from relatives and family members to larger customers such as small businesses and startups that required cloud services. He expanded the team and once the team was large enough, Jack starting branching out CloudHack Technologies’ services, eventually gaining large customers such as large business and global enterprises to where we are today.
